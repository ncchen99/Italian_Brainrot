Cappuccino Assassino is an Italian Brainrot character created by  @alexey_pigeon  on the 5th of March, 2025. He is a coffee cup with two eyes, arms wielding with katanas and legs. He wears a Naruto forehead protector and has an angry expression. He appears in a night setting, with buildings in the background and a full moon in the distance. He is a sneaky killer who attack victims after dusk[1]. His wife is Ballerina Cappuccina.  He is also known by his nickname "Capu".

The word "Cappuccino" comes from the coffee drink "cappuccino". And the word "Assassino" comes from the english word "assassin". This summarizes his appearance, being an anthromorphic cappuccino cup assassin.

Cappu-cappu-cappuccino Assassino! Assassini cappuccini! Questo killer furtivo si infiltra tra i nemici approffitando de la notte. Attento, oddiatore di caffè! Se non bevi una tazza di cappuccino al mattino, è meglio non incrociare questo tizio.

Cappu-cappu-cappuccino Assassino! Cappuccino assassins! This stealthy killer infiltrates enemies at night. Beware, coffee hater! If you don't drink a cup of cappuccino in the morning, you better not cross paths with this guy.

Cappuccino Assassino has become one of the more popular Italian Brainrot characters. He was the first of many characters based on coffee, which inspired further creations with the same theme such as Ballerina Cappuccina, who has since become more popular than him.

He has appeared in Steal a Brainrot and Plants vs Brainrots.

Despite being perfectly suited for fighting, not many battle videos inolving Cappuccino Assassino have been made. When he is used, the character uses his katanas as his main weapon. He is able to make the swords stronger by setting them on fire.  Because of that hot sword, the blade melts any material, so it makes it easier for Capu to decapu or slice his enemies.
