Ballerina Cappuccina is an Italian Brainrot character introduced on the 19th of March, 2025 by TikTok user @aironicfun. She is the wife  of Cappuccino Assassino. She can usually be seen dancing or singing. In an older iteration of the meme, she could be seen pirouetting gracefully, and she is one of the first Italian brainrots. She is often depicted as being rivals with Espressona Signora. It is inspired by the brainrot Cappuccino Assassino, who came before her.

Ballerina Cappuccina sits among the most well-known characters of Italian Brainrot, with the most popular video having reached over 50 million views on TikTok[1]. Many stories and additional characters (including characters like Espressona Signora and Ballerino Lololo) have been created thanks to her, many of which have gained mainstream recognition.

Ballerina Cappuccina is officially confirmed to be the wife of Cappucino Assassino, another very popular Italian brainrot. In the lyrics, it mentions Ballerino Lololo, who isn’t officially confirmed to be a separate brainrot and could just depict her love for ballet, however many fan made depictions shows Ballerina Cappuccina having an affair with him.

Similarily, Espressona Signora is depicted as attempting to seduce Cappuccino Assassino and succeeding, causing him to have an affair with her. Again, this is not officially confirmed as canon, yet it is frequently depicted in fanmade stories as so, even more than the theory with Ballerino Lololo.
